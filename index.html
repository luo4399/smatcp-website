Study Design and Data Generation

A comprehensive multi-omics profiling across 13 tissues from 68 Chinese donors.

Project Overview

The Somatic Mutations Across human Tissues of Chinese Project (SMaTCP), as part of The Genotype Tissues-Plasma Omics Project (GTOP), is an initiative to build the most comprehensive functional genomics atlas of somatic mosaicism in East Asian populations.

By integrating multi-omics data across multiple normal tissues at both bulk and single-cell resolutions, GTOP provides a systematic framework for characterizing genetic regulatory effects on gene expression and other molecular phenotypes.

All data are generated with sample-matched multi-omics strategy to enable precise cross-tissue somatic mutation calling.
